# What's new in Scout

The newest version is at the top. Open the **Links** tab and press
**Check for updates** any time.

## 1.1.0 — safer updates
- Updates now finish cleanly: close Scout and reopen it from the Desktop
  icon and the new version loads (before, the old one could linger)
- Bank linking works on Mac
- Hardened the updater so it can never touch your keys, accounts, or data

## 1.0.0 — first family release
- Markets, Scans, Trainer (chart-reading practice), Budget, and Links
- Paper options account support (your own Alpaca paper keys)
- One-press updates: Scout can now keep itself current
